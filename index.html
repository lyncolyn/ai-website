🌟 What Is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is the science of making machines smart—able to learn, solve problems, and make decisions like humans. It uses data and algorithms to find patterns and make predictions, like how Netflix knows what shows you’ll like.

🧠 How Is AI Different From Regular Computers?

  • Traditional computers follow exact rules: “If A happens, do B.”
  • AI can learn from experience and get better over time.
  • It doesn’t just follow instructions—it finds patterns and adapts.

🕰️ A Quick History of AI

Here are some big moments in AI history:

  • 1950: Alan Turing proposes the idea of a machine that can “think.”
  • 1956: The term “Artificial Intelligence” is born.
  • 1997: IBM’s Deep Blue beats the world chess champion.
  • 2011: IBM’s Watson wins Jeopardy!
  • 2020s: ChatGPT, smart cars, and AI art become mainstream.

🔍 Where Can You Find AI Today?

  • Phones: Voice assistants and face unlock
  • Streaming: Recommendations on Netflix or YouTube
  • Healthcare: Diagnosing diseases with AI tools
  • Self-driving cars: Making roads smarter
  • Creative tools: AI writing, music, and art

⚖️ Why It’s Important to Learn About AI
